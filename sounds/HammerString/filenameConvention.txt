Filename convention:

hs_<method>_N_<Nvalue>.wav

hs: hammer-string
method: QSA or IMP
alphaValue: value of alpha (power law exponent)
N: value of N (number of QSA segments)

Example: QSA_alpha_1.5_N_5 means the sound example generated with the QSA method for alpha = 1.5 and N = 5

IMP_64OS: IMP scheme with 64x oversampling  (decimated to 44.1kHz)
